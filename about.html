GlobeTrotter Store is a dynamic and user-friendly e-commerce platform designed for adventurous travelers and everyday explorers. The project showcases a variety of travel-related products such as backpacks, guides, accessories, and gear, each presented with interactive features like variant selection, live pricing updates, customer reviews, and detailed descriptions. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, the site demonstrates full client-server interaction with database-driven content, user-friendly navigation, and contextual help. The goal is to create an immersive shopping experience that is both visually engaging and technically robust, simulating the kind of product catalog and purchasing flow you'd expect from a modern online retail site.
